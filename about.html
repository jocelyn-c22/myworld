About

Hello! I’m Jocelyn Chen, a junior at NYU, majoring in Media, Culture, and Communication, and minoring in Business of Entertainment, Media, and Technology. I am passionate about marketing and advertising, specifically in the media, entertainment, and fashion industries. I grew up in Taipei, Taiwan, and came to NYC for college. I spent a semester abroad in Prague, immersing myself in the Czech culture. My interests include building LEGOs, listening to music, and watching rom-coms!

From Taipei to New York, and a detour to Prague, I have gained memorable experiences everywhere I go. My knowledge in digital marketing, market research, and event planning has allowed me to try different things and refine my hard and soft skills. My creativity, resilience, and open-heartedness make me a valuable member of a team.

Through traveling, I immersed myself in different cultures, gaining insights into the changes in activities and customs. Internships in different countries help me understand the different consumer behaviours and explore the various methods to target each group.
